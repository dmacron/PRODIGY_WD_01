How You Can Help

  • Adopt: Give a loving home to a cat or dog in need.
  • Donate: Your donation helps us provide food, medical care, and shelter for the animals we rescue.
  • Volunteer: Join our team and help us with rescues, fostering, or animal care
  • Foster: Become a foster parent and provide temporary care for animals before they find a forever home.

About Us

At PAWS, we are dedicated to rescuing cats and dogs from shelters, providing them with medical care, food, and a loving foster home until they find their forever families. Our mission is to reduce the number of abandoned and abused animals in our community and promote the well-being of all animals
